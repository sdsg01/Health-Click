Demistifying Health Insurance


Types of Health Insurance Policies

  • Indemnity Plans

    • Indemnity plans are traditional health insurance plans that cover your hospitalization expenses up to the sum insured.

  • Defined-Benefit Plans

    • Defined-Benefit Plans compensate the patient with a lump sum amount on the detection of illness.


Indemnity Plans
  1. Mediclaim

    2. Compensates you for hospitalization expenses incurred due to illness or accidental stay

  2. Individual Coverage

    3. Individual is entitled to receive the incurred amount during a hospitalization

  3. Family Floater

    4. Insurance of your complete family is covered under this plan. Sum insured is equally divided among the members.

  4. Unit Linked

    5. Investment cum Insurance plans

  5. Group Mediclaim

    6. Typically purchased by employers to cover the employees working in their organization.


Defined-Benefit Plans
  1. Critical Illness

    2. This plan is especially designed to treat certain specific illnesses, typically illnesses arising from lifestyle.

  2. Personal Accident

    3. This policy covers against an accidental injury or death while driving by paying a lump sum amount due to either death or permanent loss of income due to disability

  3. Hospitalization Cash Benefit

    4. Insured is paid with a daily cash allowance upto a certain limit, apart from hospital expenses.
