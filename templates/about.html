Who are We?


HealthClick is a telemedicine initiative taken to provide medicine at the patient's doorstep using technology as a bridge for handling the communication. We are a movement set to revolutionize medicine by using technology to fulfill the existing gaps and provide solutions.


Our Goals

  1. To make lab-test sampling a smoother process
  2. To deliver medicines to the comfort of users' homes
  3. To lessen the burden on healthcare officials by helping to determine the severity of a patient's symptoms
  4. To automate delivery of medicines which are needed regularly
  5. To use technology for scheduling of appointments
  6. To remind patients to take their medicines punctually through notifications


Our Motto


To strive, to seek, to find and not to yield in providing quality service through the fusion of technology and medicine sectors.
